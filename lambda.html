Dead Letter Queue

When you invoke a function asynchronously, Lambda sends the event to a queue. A separate process reads events from the queue and runs your function.

  • Asynchronously invoked lambda functions are retried twice before being discarded
  • Unprocessed events can be sent to an SNS or SQS DLQ
